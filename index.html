The 5 Most Anticipated Games of 2020

Persona 5   Cyberpunk 2077   Final Fantasy 7   The Last of US II   Half-Life Alyx

1. Persona 5 Royal

Go to top.

Known for it's unique gameplay and stylistic choices. Atlus promises to add an all new section of the story as well as as an entirely new character in the re-release of this 2016 masterpiece. While DLC from previous versions won't be carrying over, the content promised by Atlus makes it worth the 80$ price-tag. Royal was released in October of 2019 in Japan and is set to be released in the west sometime in 2020.

2. Cyberpunk 2077

Go to top.

Based off the 1988 Tabletop RPG of the same name, Cyberpunk 2077 promises to allow fans of the classic game to experience it in an all new light, while staying true to the RPG roots that made it so successful. With original creator Mike Pondsmith's involvement and talent such as Keanu Reeves being cast play characters from the game, CD Project Red is sure to provide an exceptional product.

3. Final Fantasy 7 Remake

Go to top.

After over 20 years since the release of FF7 on the original Playstation, Square Enix plans to take the classic story to new heights with this remake. Releasing exclusively on PS4, it plans on providing a unique take on a classic story that still resides with people to this day.

4. The Last of US Part II

Go to top.

After the critically acclaimed masterpiece that was the original Last of US on the PS3, Naughty Dog is finally getting ready to release the sequel to a Playstation 3 classic. Secheduled for a Febuary 21st release, The Last of US II is sure to be one of the best games of 2020.

5. Half-Life Alyx

Go to top.

After over 10 years without releasing a new installment, Valve finally announces another installment to the half-life series. Taking place sometime before the events of Half-Life 2, players will control Gordon Freeman's companion Alyx Vance and experience the half-life universe in a way that has never been seen before. Even though it will be a VR exclusive, many people are calling it the most anticipated game of 2020, despite it's early release in March of 2020.
